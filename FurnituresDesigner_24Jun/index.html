How to use our product configurator.



Please remember it's important to follow next steps consecutively to easily build your ideal configuration:



1️. Start from selecting base frame Width / Height / Depth (right menu’s top)

2️. Then add and correct any necessary Vertical Dividers (right menu’s middle)

3️. Horizontal dividers comes next, choose amount and positions you need (left menu’s top)

4️. After base frame dimensions and horizontal dividers applied, your can add internal shelving and doors (left menu’s middle)



Colorise parts whenever you want from the right side menu. You can also hide doors from top right round icons to see shelving.


Don’t forget to check dimensions and price before you proceed to order
