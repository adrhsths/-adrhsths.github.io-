How to use our product configurator.



Please remember it's important to follow next steps consecutively to easily build your ideal configuration:



1. Start from selecting base frame Width / Height / Depth (right menus top)

2. Then add and correct any necessary Vertical Dividers (right menus middle)

3. Horizontal dividers comes next, choose amount and positions you need (left menus top)

4. After base frame dimensions and horizontal dividers applied, your can add internal shelving and doors (left menus middle)



Colorise parts whenever you want from the right side menu. You can also hide doors from top right round icons to see shelving.


Dont forget to check dimensions and price before you proceed to order
